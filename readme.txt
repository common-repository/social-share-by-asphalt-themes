== Description ==

A really simple, Gutenberg Ready, social share plugin for WordPress posts. Social share platforms - Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+ and Reddit. Once activated below every WordPress posts you will see social share options for that post. 


[Plugin demo](https://resumeepro.asphaltthemes.com/2018/05/26/post-three/)

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= where are the plugin settings? =

Once activated go to Settings->Social Share


= where will the social share icons appear? =

Social share icons will appear below every WordPress posts.


= Plans for more social sharing options? =

Possibly, not.

= Why my social handles style is different? =

May be the theme you are using overriding their own styles.
